ifconfig stands for interface configurations used to view and change the configuration of network interface in your system

ifconfig  
  eth0 -> you are connected with wire
  wlan0 -> you are conneted wireless
we will get mac-address, lan-address(IPB4-address),mask-address,broadcast-address,IPB6-address

sudo ifconfig eth0 down  ---disconnects your internet
sudo ifconfig eth0 up    ---connects to internet

use (man ifconfig)
